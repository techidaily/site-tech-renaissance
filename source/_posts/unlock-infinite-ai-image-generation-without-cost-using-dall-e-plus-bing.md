##  How Bing Image Creator and Its "Boosts" Work

 Hot on the heels of releasing [AI Bing Chat](https://facebook-video-recording.techidaily.com/new-in-2024-the-insiders-look-at-securing-fb-status-videos/) in February 2023, [Microsoft paired it with a DALL-E-powered image generator in March 2023](https://review-topics.techidaily.com/recover-excel-2013-files-from-virus-infected-pen-drives-for-free-by-stellar-guide/).
