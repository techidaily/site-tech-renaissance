## Comprehensive Tutorial on Converting KFX Books to Popular eBooks (EPUB/PDF/AZW3/MOBI)

## How to Convert KFX Books to EPUB/PDF/AZW3/Mobi

![follow](http://www.epubor.com/images/follow.png)

As the Amazon wants to build its own digital ecosystem, employing **Kindle KFX DRM** to all books becomes a trend. Since more and more Kindle eBooks have been encrypted with Kindle KFX DRM, [Epubor Ultimate](https://www.epubor.com/) has been released as the first tool on the market to remove Kindle KFX DRM. To read Kindle KFX books on more reading apps or devices, we need to convert KFX format to other widely-used formats like EPUB and PDF format.

 In this article, I am going to tell you 3 solutions on how to convert KFX books to DRM-free EPUB/AZW3/Mobi/PDF formats.

* [Solution 1\. Convert KFX Books Downloaded via Kindle for PC/MAC](https://tools.techidaily.com/epubor/products/)
* [Solution 2\. Convert KFX Books Downloaded from Amazon Website](https://tools.techidaily.com/epubor/products/)
* [Solution 3\. Convert KFX Books Downloaded via Kindle E-ink Device](https://tools.techidaily.com/epubor/products/)

### Solution 1\. Convert KFX Books Downloaded via Kindle for PC/MAC

This is the most universal method, no matter you have a Kindle device or not. You just need a Kindle desktop reading app, and make sure you can read your books on your computer first.

First download Epubor Ultimate for Win/Mac to your computer.

Download Epubor Ultimate for A Free Trial

[](https://tools.techidaily.com/epubor/ultimate/) [](https://tools.techidaily.com/epubor/ultimate/) 

#### **Step 1\. Download Kindle for PC V2.0 or Kindle for Mac V1.40**.

[_Kindle for PC V2.4.0 Download_](https://download.epubor.com/KindleForPC-installer-2.4.0-70904.exe
) [_Kindle for Mac V1.40 Download_](https://download.epubor.com/KindleForMac-installer-1.40.dmg)

#### **Step2\. Download Kindle KFX books via Kindle for PC/Mac**

Run the Kindle for PC/Mac and sign in with your Amazon account. Then all your Kindle books will be synced to the library automatically. Right-click on the books to download them to your computer. 

![download Kindle books to computer](http://www.epubor.com/images/uppic/download-kindle-books-to-your-computer.png)

#### **Step3\. Remove DRM from Kindle KFX books**

Run [Epubor Ultimate](https://tools.techidaily.com/epubor/ultimate/), usually if your downloaded books are saved in the default folder "**My Kindle Content**", they will be automatically detected by this software. You can see them under the "Kindle" tab at the left column. A really user-friendly design. No need to find books everywhere manually.

Next, what we need to do is to just drag and drop Kindle books from the left side to the right blank zone. Then the DRM will be removed in a second with "Decrypted" showing.

![convert kindle formats to epub](http://www.epubor.com/images/uppic/convert-kindle-formats-to-epub.png)

#### **Step4\. Convert KFX to EPUB/PDF/Mobi/AZW3**

After we have removed Kindle DRM successfully, now we can convert the DRM-free books to EPUB/PDF/Mobi/AZW3\. To convert the decrypted Kindle files to EPUB/PDF/Mobi/AZW3, just choose the format you want as the output format, then click "Convert" button to start conversion.

![convert kfx to epub/pdf/mobi/azw3](http://www.epubor.com/images/uppic/convert-kindle-to-epub.png)

When the conversion has been finished, you will see a "**Succeeded**" on the right side of your added books.

![](http://www.epubor.com/images/faq.png) Failed to remove DRM from KCR files(on Mac Only)

If you are on Mac, some kindle books are downloaded as kcr format. Epubor Ultimate can detect and display the number of these books. See below picture. 

![kindle kcr format](https://epubor.com/images/uppic/ultimate-show-kcr-ebooks.png)

To decrypt and convert these books, please follow this guide>>[_How to remove drm from kindle kcr and convert kcr to pdf_](https://tools.techidaily.com/epubor/products/). 

### Solution 2\. Convert KFX books Downloaded from Amazon Website

Besides Solution 1, there is another useful way to convert KFX books to EPUB/AZW3/Mobi/PDF.

#### Step1\. Download purchased books from Amazon website

Enter Amazon website, log in it with your Amazon account. Then hover over the "**Your Account**" drop-down menu, select "**Manage Your Content and Devices**", you can see all your Kindle content here. Just find the KFX books you have downloaded via Kindle device, then tap the button "**More Actions**", click "**Download & transfer via USB**".

![download kfx books from amazon website](http://www.epubor.com/images/uppic/manage-your-content-devices.png)
