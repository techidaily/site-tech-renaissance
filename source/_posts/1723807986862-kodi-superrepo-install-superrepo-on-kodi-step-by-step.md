## Video Editor 1: iMovie (Free)  

 iMovie is an official Apple video editor. It’s free and intended for amateur users. With the easy-to-use interface, you can use it to edit your videos easily.  

 Here are some of the features that iMovie offers:  

**Adding Music**

 iMovie allows you to add music to your videos. You can select the music from the iTune library, the iMovie built-in sound library, or GarageBand. Adding music to make the move sound as good as it looks.

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/03/image-717.png)

**Adding Title**

 iMovie offers dozens of styles for you to add animated titles and credits to your videos. You can select the titles to add according to your needs.

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/03/image-718.png)

**Cutting Clips**

 Cutting clips would be the basic requirements for video editing. iMovie also allows you to cut the videos into clips.

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/03/image-719.png)

 Change the video speed  

 You can change the speed of the action shots so the video clips can play fast or slow.

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/03/image-720.png)
  
**TIP** : Apple doesn’t offer a complete manual for you to follow to use the features. ### How to Edit Videos with iMovie on Mac  

 1)**Open iMovie from Launchpad** . If iMovie isn’t installed on your Mac, you can[download](https://support.apple.com/downloads/imovie) and install it manually.

 2)**Click** **Create New** and select**Movie** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/03/image-721.png)

 3)**Click the import icon** at the upper-left corner then select the video clip you want to edit. You can select the video clip from the drive or the folder where you store the video clips.

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/03/image-722.png)

 4)**Drag the video clip to the timeline** and select the specific feature to add the effects you want to the videos.

 5)**Export the video** to local drive or upload the video to social media like YouTube and Facebook.

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/03/image-723.png)

**Other information**

 You not only can edit one video clip, you can combine different video clips. Just import the video clips and drag them to the timeline and add the effects you want.  

 iMovie is easier to use with the new Macbook Pro, as the most useful commands automatically appear on the keyboard. You can just use the keys on the keyboard to use the important features like Add to Movie, Add as Overlay.  

 iMovie is compatible with all Apple devices. It means you can edit the same video on different Apple devices. For example, if you start the editing on iPhone, you can continue the editing on Mac.  

 The exported file will only be in MOV format. If you want to export the video to other formats, you can choose to use Video Editor 2.
