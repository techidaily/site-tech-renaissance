## Mastering Privacy: Stealth Browsing Techniques for Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari & Opera
### What to Know

* Press**Ctrl** +**Shift** +**N** or**Ctrl** +**Shift** +**P** to go incognito in most browsers.
* Use the app's menu to open incognito mode from the mobile app.
* Incognito hides your browsing history. Your IP address does not change.

 This article explains how to open incognito mode for private browsing in Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari, and Opera.

 FAQ

* What's the benefit of turning on private browsing?  
 Private browsing prevents other users on your device from seeing your internet history. It also helps to block websites from tracking your online activity with[cookies](https://www.lifewire.com/web-browser-cookies-3483129) . Therefore, you're unlikely to see online ads related to websites you visit during private browsing sessions.
* How do I put a password on my browser on Android?  
 You can[lock apps on Android](https://www.lifewire.com/lock-apps-android-4583099) with your device's security code or use a third-party app. You can also[set up Android parental controls](https://www.lifewire.com/childproof-android-4137048) to childproof your device.
