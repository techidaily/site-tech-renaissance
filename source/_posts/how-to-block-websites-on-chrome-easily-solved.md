## Way 1: Using the Restricted sites feature in your Chrome settings

 The most direct way to block a website is to add it to the Restricted sites in your Chrome. This way requires no extra extension or third-party software.

It’s easy to set:

1) Open Chrome on your computer.

2) Click the more options button ![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/img_5a1b8c9677c43.png) on the top right of Chrome. Then select Settings.

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/img_5a1b8c82821d1.png)

 3) Click **Advanced**  at the bottom.

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/img_5a1b8d440c31d.png)

 4) Click **Open proxy settings**  in the **System**  section.

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/img_5a1b8d70903c8.png)

 5) Click the **Security**  tab, then select **Restricted sites**  \> **Sites** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/img_5a1b8e022cb18.jpg)

 6) Enter the site you want to block, then click **Add** . The site should then be listed in the Websites box. Click **Close** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/img_5a1b8f45b754e.png)

 7）Click **OK** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/img_5a1b93f2949ed.jpg)

 8) Clear you browsing data on Chrome, then restart Chrome. You can’t access the website you set then.

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/img_5a2b44f5ac0ac.png)

**Note:**  Next time when you want to access the websites you’ve blocked, enter this Restricted sites window through the same step 1) -5), then click the site in the Websites box, and **Remove** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/img_5a1b8f2c21d4b.png)

## Way 2: Editing your Windows Hosts file

 If you’re familiar with using computer and you’re on Windows, you can also block websites through editing your Windows Host file.**Note:** The websites are blocked on the specific computer from all the browsers.

Please follow these:

 1) Copy the following path and paste in the search box from the Start menu.

**C:\\Windows\\system32\\drivers\\etc**

 2) On your keyboard, press **Enter** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/img_5a1bb18d984c0.png)

 3) Right-click on the **hosts**  file, then click **Open with** , select **Notepad** . If you have **Notepad++** , choose it like as I do.

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/img_5a1bb2245a4a8.png)

 4) On the bottom of the file, type 127.0.0.1 and add the website name you want to block after one space. For example, if you want to block the Facebook website on Chrome, type this: 127.0.0.1 <www.facebook.com>

**Important:**  Remember not to put in a # before 127.0.0.1\. If there’re other websites you want to block, enter your 2nd address after 127.0.0.2, and so on.

 5) Run Chrome on your computer and type the website address you set, you should then find it can’t be accessed.

**Note:**  Next time when you want to access the websites you’ve blocked, open your hosts file through the same step 1) -3) and delete the number and website.
