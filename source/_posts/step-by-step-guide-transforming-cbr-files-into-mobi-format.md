CBR is a format which stores images and usually used in digital comics. As we all know Kindle devices and related software are good tools to read comic. ![CBR TO MOBI](http://www.epubor.com/images/uppic/CBR-TO-MOBI.png)The disappointing fact is that CBR is not supported by Kindle. And you need to convert **CBR to Mobi** so that the comics can be read in your Kindle devices. You can find the answer in following contents.

Usually, the size of CBR comic files is bigger than Mobi files which with same contents. After converting CBR to Kindle you can store bigger quantities of comics whether in computer or Kindle reading devices. Following ways are all done for you.

### Method 1\. CBR to Mobi Converter Software: Calibre

Calibre is so popular for e-readers and it is totally free to be used. Its rich functions will meet your needs. As a desktop software, Calibre can solve most problems for your digital reading life.

#### Step 1: Download & Install Calibre

Calibre software has many versions for different installed environment. Click [here](https://calibre-ebook.com/download) to choose the right version to download and install it.

![Get Calibre](http://www.epubor.com/images/uppic/Get-Calibre.png)

#### Step 2: Adding CBR Files to Calibre

After installing Calibre, Select CBR files, drag and drop them to Calibre interface. Or you can click "Add books" button in the navigation bar.

![Calibre adding books](http://www.epubor.com/images/uppic/calibre-adding-books.png)

#### Step 3: Convert CBR Files to Mobi

Select CBR files and right click it. You can see convert button in tap control. Of course, you can also select "Convert books" button in top navigation bar. From displayed options, bulk convert option can batch convert files quickly. Choose the suitable option right for you.

![select right convert option](http://www.epubor.com/images/uppic/select-right-convert-option.png)

Select Mobi as your output format and click OK. Then you can see the converting progress at the bottom of the interface. When the "Jobs" record turns run "0", you can find the converted Mobi books in your local Calibre Library of your PC.

![cbr to mobi format](http://www.epubor.com/images/uppic/cbr-to-mobi-format.png)

**PROS:** free; batch conversion; more convenient functions; no limitations about file size

**CONS:** need to download & install software
