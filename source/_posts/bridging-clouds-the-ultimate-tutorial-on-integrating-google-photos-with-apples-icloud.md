## Bridging Clouds: The Ultimate Tutorial on Integrating Google Photos with Apple's iCloud

Close 

###  What to Know

* First, you need to export your Google Photos files. There's no way to copy Google Photos to iCloud directly.
* Google Takeout is the best way to download everything at once. You can also save individual items only.
* To move Google Photos to iCloud, visit**iCloud.com/photos** and upload the photos and videos you saved.

 This article explains how to export your photos from Google Photos and then import them directly into iCloud.

##  Is There a Way to Transfer Google Photos to iCloud? 

 The short answer is yes, but not directly. There's no simple transfer button to magically move everything over to iCloud from Google Photos. Your only option is to download the files you want to import to iCloud and then upload them to your account. Below are all the directions you need to get this done.

##  How to Download From Google Photos 

 Currently, the only way to move your Google Photos over is to download them to your computer and then upload them to iCloud. There are three ways to do this, depending on how many items you want to copy over.

###  Export Specific Photos and Videos Only 

 Don't want to transfer_all_ your Google Photos data? We mentioned how to export certain albums above, but you can also download each item you want to put in iCloud. Here's how it works from the Google Photos website:

1. [ Open Google Photos](https://photos.google.com/) in your web browser.
2. Find the photos you want to export and select them using the small checkmark in the top corner of the images. Alternatively, you can mark all for export by selecting the top left-most photo and holding Shift while you scroll to the bottom of the page.  
![Where to select an individual picture to export in Google Photos.](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/RQWYARjBtZ9r35sU-1v0TPcMWjk=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/B2-TransferGooglePhotostoiCloud-annotated-63fc73b85b9b4e6fb06557561b0e2dd3.jpg)
3. Press**Shift** +**d** on your keyboard. Or, go to the**three-dot menu** and choose**Download** .

### Export From Your Phone 

 To grab certain photos from the app on your phone, follow these steps:  

1. Long-press the first item you want to save, then tap any other photos or videos you plan to transfer to iCloud.
2. Tap the**Share** button.
3. Select an app to send the photos through, like your email or a messaging service you can access on a computer.

##  What Is the Best Way to Move My Google Photos Into iCloud? 

 With your Google Photos exported, you can now import them into iCloud. The easiest way to import photos into iCloud is through the iCloud website. Here's how:

1. [ Open iCloud Photos](https://www.icloud.com/photos/) and log in with your Apple ID.
2. From the**Library** tab, select the**cloud** icon on the right.  
![The Library tab and upload button in iCloud Photos](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/HQtHaQvdrjVYJyeCB4yk96rz4iE=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/icloud-photos-7e448b16dd48483ab3624824f38edc91.png)
3. Find the folder you exported your Google Photos items to and then choose what to import into iCloud.  
![Two photos selected for upload to iCloud](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/NviFEx3x3YwtVuNJRpaJ-_qfBzk=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/icloud-images-uploaded-6a62cfdd9ea8486189ed8c52c7054fe5.png)  
 You might have to do some consolidation to make all your Google Photos files exist in the same folder for easy upload.

 FAQ

* How do I transfer Google Photos to my gallery?  
 You can restore items from Google Photos to an Android phone's gallery app through**Library** \>**Trash** in Google Photos. Select the photos you're moving, and then select**Restore** . The item will return to the folders it used to be in, including your gallery.
* How do I transfer Google Photos to my computer?  
 You can move Google Photos to your computer's hard drive by downloading them from the website. Sign in to Google Photos, and then hover your cursor over the ones you want to move and select the**checkbox** . Once you've highlighted all of the items you want, click**menu** \>**Download** .
