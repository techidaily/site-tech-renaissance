 FAQ

* How many Indiana Jones movies are there?  
 There are five theatrical releases:_Indiana Jones and the Temple of Doom_ ,_Indiana Jones and the Raiders of the Lost Ark_ ,_Indiana Jones and the Last Crusade_ ,_Indiana Jones and the Kingdom of the Crystal Skull_ ._and Indiana Jones and the Dial of Destiny._ There were 21 movies released on video edited from_The Young Indiana Jones Chronicles_ TV series.
* Where were the Indiana Jones movies filmed?  
_Raiders of the Lost Ark_ was filmed in the U.S., Tunisia, France, and England._Indiana Jones and the Temple of Doom_ was filmed in Sri Lanka, China, the U.S., and England._Indiana Jones and the Last Crusade_ was filmed in Spain, West Germany, and England._Indiana Jones and the Kingdom of the Crystal Skull_ was filmed in various U.S. locations, and_Indiana Jones and the Dial of Destiny_ shot in England, Scotland, Italy, and Morocco.
* What is Indiana Jones scared of?  
 Indiana Jones has ophidiophobia—an extreme fear of snakes. In 1912, he fell into a crate of snakes on the Dunn and Duffy Circus train. Indy was already scared of snakes, but this terrifying event triggered his ophidiophobia.
