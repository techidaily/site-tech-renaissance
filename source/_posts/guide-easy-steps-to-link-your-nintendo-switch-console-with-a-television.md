###  What to Know

* Open the back cover of the Nintendo Switch dock and connect both the AC adapter and HDMI cable.
* Plug the other ends of the AC adapter into a wall outlet and the HDMI cable into your TV.
* Detach the Joy-Cons, place your Nintendo Switch into the dock, and power it on.

 This article explains the steps to connect your Nintendo Switch or Nintendo Switch (OLED model) to your television. Note: The Nintendo Switch Lite does not support TV mode.

## What You’ll Need 

 Gather these items to connect your Nintendo Switch to your TV. Everything except for the TV should have been[ included in the box when you purchased your Switch](https://www.lifewire.com/what-comes-with-the-nintendo-switch-5113752) .

* Nintendo Switch game unit with Joy-Cons
* Nintendo Switch dock
* AC adapter
* HDMI cable
* Joy-Coy grip or wrist straps (optional)
* HDMI-compatible TV

##  How to Connect Your Nintendo Switch to Your TV 

 Once you have all the materials above, connecting your Switch to your television takes only a minute.

1. Place your Nintendo Switch dock on a stable surface close to the television. Open the back cover of the dock.
2. Connect the AC adapter to the **AC Adapter** port, if it’s not already, and plug it into a wall outlet.
3. Plug one end of the HDMI cable into the **HDMI Out** port on the back of the dock and the other end into the HDMI port on the back of your TV.

##  How to Disconnect From the TV 

 If you want to disconnect your Nintendo Switch from your television when you finish playing, reverse the steps above.

 You can then plug the dock back into the wall outlet, reattach the Joy-Cons, and put the unit into the dock to[ charge the Nintendo Switch](https://www.lifewire.com/charge-nintendo-switch-4136834) and its controllers.
