## How Do I Unhide Hidden Apps?

 You may have[hidden apps on your iPhone](https://www.lifewire.com/hide-apps-on-iphone-8584258) in the past because you weren't using them often or you just like a few tidy Home screens.

 Follow these steps to locate a hidden app in the App Library and return it to one of your Home screens.

1. Swipe from right to left on the**Home** screen to Open the App Library.  
 It might be a few screens over, so keep swiping until you see_App Library_ in a search bar at the top of the screen.
